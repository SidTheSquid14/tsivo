Verbs

There are 3 main types of verbs. There are linking verbs, helping verbs, and action verbs. Action verbs express something you can do, an action. Linking verbs usually express a state of being. Helping verbs are found in verb phrases, and they usually just help other verbs. There are some examples below.
  • Action Verb: Billy PUNCHED his computer screen after losing a Cool Math Game.
  • Linking Verb: Mr. Tsivourakis IS cool.
  • Helping Verb: I HAVE been secretly playing Pokemon Go on my Kindle Fire.
